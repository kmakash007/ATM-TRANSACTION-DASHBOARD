The Power BI dashboard visualizes ATM withdrawal patterns across various locations. 
The key metrics analyzed include the total number of withdrawals, amounts withdrawn 
by different card types, and daily trends segmented by weekdays and holidays. 
By analyzing these factors, the dashboard provides actionable insights for optimizing ATM 
operations, improving cash management, and enhancing customer service efficiency based on 
usage patterns. This data-driven approach can help banks and financial institutions in 
strategic decision-making.
